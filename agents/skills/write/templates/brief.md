# Brief — <Topic>

**For:** <reader> · **From:** <author> · **Updated:** YYYY-MM-DD

## Background
What the reader needs to know to follow the rest. Two or three short paragraphs. Don't assume context they don't have, don't pad context they do.

## Question
The decision or judgment this brief is meant to inform. State it as a question.

## Options
Two to four options. For each:
- One-line description
- Pros (1–3)
- Cons (1–3)

## Recommendation
Which option, and why. Be direct. If you don't have a recommendation, say so and explain what you'd need to form one.

## Next step
What happens after the reader decides.
