# People — Personal CRM

> Relationship context, interaction history, and notes about people in your life.

---

## Structure

One file per person:
```
people/
├── AGENTS.md           ← You are here
├── jane-smith.md       ← Individual person file
└── john-doe.md
```

---

## Person Template

```markdown
# [Name]

**Role:** [Their role/relationship to you]
**Organization:** [Company/context]
**Last Contact:** YYYY-MM-DD

## Context
[How you know them, shared history]

## Notes
- [Key things to remember]
- [Preferences, interests]

## Interaction Log
- YYYY-MM-DD: [Brief note about interaction]
```

---

## For Agents

- Extract people context from meeting transcripts
- Update interaction logs after meetings
- Surface relevant context before meetings with someone
- Help maintain relationship health
