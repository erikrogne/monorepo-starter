# Notes — Quick Captures

> Fleeting thoughts, quick captures, inbox items waiting to be processed.

---

## Structure

```
notes/
├── AGENTS.md     ← You are here
├── inbox/        ← Unsorted captures
└── [note-files]  ← Individual notes
```

---

## Naming Convention

```
YYYY-MM-DD_topic.md
```

Or for quick captures:
```
YYYY-MM-DD-HHMMSS.md
```

---

## What Goes Here

- Quick thoughts and ideas
- Voice memos transcribed
- Screenshots with annotations
- Clippings from web/apps
- Anything captured in the moment

---

## Processing Notes

Notes flow from raw to wiki:

1. Capture quickly here (don't overthink)
2. Review periodically
3. Extract insights to `wiki/concepts/` or `wiki/summaries/`
4. Original note stays here (immutable)

---

## For Agents

- Help process inbox items when asked
- Extract patterns and themes to wiki
- Never delete or modify notes here
- Suggest connections to existing knowledge
