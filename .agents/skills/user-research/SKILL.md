# User Research Synthesis

Turn raw research data into actionable insights.

## When to Use

- After conducting user interviews
- Analyzing survey results
- Synthesizing usability test findings
- Creating research reports

## Process

### 1. Organize Raw Data

- Collect all notes, recordings, surveys
- Tag by participant and topic
- Identify initial patterns

### 2. Find Themes

Group observations into themes:
- What keeps coming up?
- What surprised you?
- What confirms/contradicts assumptions?

### 3. Extract Insights

For each theme:
- **Observation**: What did you see/hear?
- **Interpretation**: What does it mean?
- **Implication**: What should we do?

### 4. Prioritize

Rank insights by:
- Frequency (how many users?)
- Severity (how painful?)
- Opportunity (how impactful if solved?)

### 5. Recommend Actions

- Specific, actionable recommendations
- Connect to insights
- Include quick wins and larger initiatives

## Deliverables

- Research summary (1-pager)
- Detailed findings document
- Recommendations deck
