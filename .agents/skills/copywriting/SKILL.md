# Copywriting

Write clear, compelling marketing and product copy.

## When to Use

- Writing landing page copy
- Product descriptions
- Email campaigns
- Ad copy
- Headlines and taglines
- CTAs (calls to action)

## Principles

### 1. Know Your Audience
- Who are they?
- What do they care about?
- What problem do they have?

### 2. Lead with Benefits
- Focus on outcomes, not features
- "Save 10 hours/week" > "Automated scheduling"

### 3. Be Specific
- Use numbers and details
- Avoid vague claims

### 4. Create Urgency
- Limited time offers
- Scarcity (when honest)

### 5. Clear CTA
- One primary action
- Action-oriented verbs

## Process

1. Understand the goal and audience
2. Draft multiple versions
3. Refine based on feedback
4. Test and iterate
