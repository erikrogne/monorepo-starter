# Knowledge — Context for AI Agents

This folder contains context about you and your work to help AI agents provide better assistance.

---

## Files

| File | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| `about-you.md` | Your role, projects, preferences |

---

## How Agents Use This

When you start a conversation, agents read this folder to understand:
- Who you are and what you do
- Your current projects and goals
- How you prefer to communicate
- Tools and workflows you use

---

## Best Practices

1. **Keep it current** — Update when your role or projects change
2. **Be specific** — Details help agents give better suggestions
3. **Include preferences** — Communication style, formatting, etc.

---

## Adding More Context

Create additional files for specific contexts:

- `team.md` — Team structure, stakeholders
- `projects.md` — Detailed project info
- `glossary.md` — Company-specific terms
