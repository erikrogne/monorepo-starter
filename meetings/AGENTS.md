# Meetings — Meeting Notes and Transcripts

This folder stores meeting notes, transcripts, and summaries.

---

## Structure

```
meetings/
├── AGENTS.md          ← You are here
└── [meeting-files]    ← Your meeting notes
```

---

## Naming Convention

Use consistent naming for easy search:

```
YYYY-MM-DD_meeting-name.md
```

Examples:
- `2024-02-15_product-review.md`
- `2024-02-16_design-sync.md`
- `2024-02-17_stakeholder-update.md`

---

## Meeting Note Template

```markdown
# Meeting: [Title]

**Date:** YYYY-MM-DD
**Attendees:** [Names]
**Type:** [Sync | Review | Planning | 1:1]

## Summary
[2-3 sentence summary]

## Discussion
- [Topic 1]
- [Topic 2]

## Decisions
- [ ] Decision 1
- [ ] Decision 2

## Action Items
- [ ] @person — Task (due: date)

## Notes
[Detailed notes]
```
